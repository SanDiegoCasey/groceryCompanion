About Grocery Companion

Grocery Companion makes it easey for you to keep track of the prices of your most commonly purchased items from your favorite stores!

Using the app

When you first sign up you will want to pick out your top 3 stores that you usually shop at. Click on the Shops button and choose your 3 primary stores. You can also go here at any time to make changes to your stores if you would prefer to use a different store in your top 3.

The next thing you will want to do is start adding your most common products that you typically buy by clicking the "Products" button If you don't know the prices right away, you can always add them later while your at the store or looking at this weeks flyers or ads.

Once you have a few of your items in your dataabase you can start adding them to your shopping list by clicking on the list button .

Using your list

From your list view, use the drop down to find any items that are already available to add, simply click on the check button and they will be added. If you don't have the item available that you want to add, simply click the NEW+ button around you will be taken to the add product page right away. If you choose a product but the prices are wrong, simply click the green pencil edit button and you will be taken to that products page where you can update the prices.
